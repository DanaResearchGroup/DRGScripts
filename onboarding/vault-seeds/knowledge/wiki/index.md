## Knowledge Base

The home map-of-content for this wiki. Every entity, topic, and source should be
reachable from here (directly or via a sub-index). This is the front door — start here.

See [[CLAUDE.md]] for how the wiki is built and [[learnings.md]] for what we've learned.

## How this works

Raw material lands in `knowledge/raw/`. It gets read, summarized into a **source note**,
and integrated into the **entity** and **topic** pages below — with cross-links and any
contradictions flagged. Knowledge is compiled once here and kept current, not re-derived
on every question.
